My AI Wardrobe

iOS · AI · Fashion Tech

← Scroll to see more screenshots

About the App

My AI Wardrobe is an intelligent personal stylist app that lives in your pocket. Photograph your clothing and the AI automatically detects category, color, and style — building a digital wardrobe effortlessly. Then let it suggest outfits tailored to any occasion.

Key Features

  • AI Photo Analysis — Auto-detect clothing type, color, and style from photos using Gemini AI
  • Smart Wardrobe — Browse, filter, and manage your full clothing catalog
  • Outfit Suggestions — AI-powered recommendations based on occasion and weather
  • Wardrobe Insights — Cost-per-wear stats, most/least worn items, and wardrobe gap analysis
  • Multi-language — Supports English, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, and more

Tech Stack

Swift / SwiftUI Gemini AI GraphQL AWS EC2 PostgreSQL FashionCLIP Go

WanderTogether

iOS · Travel · Group

Scroll to see more →

About the App

WanderTogether makes traveling with friends simple, organized and fun. Create trips, capture memories and keep expenses in sync — all in one shared space. No more scattered group chats, messy spreadsheets, or forgotten moments.

Key Features

  • Shared Trips — Create group trips and invite friends via a shareable link
  • Memory Capture — Add notes, photos, and locations to build a living trip journal
  • Expense Sync — Log expenses, auto-split among participants, and settle up easily
  • Export PDF — Generate a beautiful trip summary to keep or share after the journey
  • Real-time Sync — Everyone in the group stays up to date instantly

Tech Stack

Swift / SwiftUI Go MapKit AWS RDS S3 PostgreSQL OAuth

Baby Food Journal

iOS · Parenting · AI

Scroll to see more →

About the App

Baby Food Journal makes it easy for parents to track everything their little one eats. Log meals with photos, browse a curated food library, and get AI-powered diet insights — all presented in a delightful, easy-to-use calendar.

Key Features

  • Meal Logging — Track food entries with date, time, amount, and optional photos
  • Calendar Journal — Visual day-by-day history; tap any date to review and edit entries
  • Food Library — Curated baby food database plus custom food input
  • Age-based Recommendations — Food suggestions tailored to your baby's age and stage
  • AI Diet Analysis — Smart insights on nutritional balance based on logged meals
  • Baby Profiles — Google Sign-In with individual profiles per baby

Tech Stack

Swift / SwiftUI AI Analysis Google Auth AWS PostgreSQL
